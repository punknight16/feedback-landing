Tracking the pulse of your users

Why Do We Exist?

Most Analytics Tools only provide information about whether a user is visiting your page. This isn't enough information to tell whether they are reading your page or even seeing your call-to-action button. We provide detailed analytics about where a user is scrolling with a simple SDK.

How It Works

Register below to gain access to our javascript file. Then include the javascript file at the top of any html page that you want to track. Add html5 section tags to any areas of your page that you would like to track. Then the software tracks whether a user scrolls to that section and sends a heartbeat back to our servers every 10 seconds. That's it!
